"About"

WThis system has an important role in supply chain management or in other words, it is a supply chain system that will always be up to date and only certain employees have access, in accordance with regulations. Warehouse is an integral part of the modern supply chain that involves the process of sourcing, producing, and distributing goods or products. Starting from raw materials, processing semi-finished products to finished products..

To coordinate the distribution of goods resulting from the imbalance between supply and demand, a warehouse will be very important. Inventories of these goods require space such as temporary storage facilities called warehouses. In managing warehouse management, there are several principles, namely the speed of market delivery and meeting demand, accuracy in running the supply chain continuously, customers can get products easily and quickly, getting information related to inventory in the warehouse is easier.

Our Benefit

Warehouse itself has various important benefits that can support the company's operational activities. The following is an explanation of what are the benefits of having a warehouse:

1. Increase Effectiveness Effectiveness that allows customers or users to easily access or obtain company-owned products.

2. Reduce Expenditure They don't really need all the equipment that the company uses manually so they can reduce business operational costs. Because the warehouse they have created is structured.

3. Faster Business Process One of the company's competitive strategies is to supply products as close to the market as possible so that the marketing or delivery process can be faster.

4. Increased Customer Satisfaction Increase customer satisfaction because in doing the preparation of goods does not require a long time.

Warehouse Function stock inventory management software Warehouse has a function other than as a place to store goods but also has several other functions. There are four warehouse functions, namely:

1. Goods Storage The main function of the warehouse is as a place to store goods. In storing goods, it must be neat and according to the plan so that in the retrieval process there is no confusion, especially in warehouses that accommodate a lot of goods.

2. As Product Safety Furthermore, as a security for inventory or goods sent, and orders to remain intact they can be reused. The function of the warehouse is not only in terms of theft prevention but disaster mitigation.

3. As a Packaging Maker The third function is as a packing builder, the company will use the warehouse as an assembly facility or a place for packing and bundling. Semi-finished goods or products can be perfected in the warehouse and then sent to customers.

4. To Increase Inventory and Re-Order One of the difficulties in managing the warehouse is determining the time to reorder goods because the available stock of goods is in various warehouse locations.

Our Method

Method of Storage of Goods in Warehouse You can do storage in the warehouse in five ways, as follows: 1. Dedicated storage The first way to store goods in the warehouse is the dedicated storage or fixed location method. This method of storing goods in the warehouse means that each product has a fixed place or location in storage in the warehouse. The location cannot be changed or used by other types of products even if there is an empty space.

2. Randomized Storage Second, there is a method of randomized storage or floating lot storage, which is a way of storing goods which is the opposite of the dedicated storage method. The randomized storage method means that storage in the warehouse can be done randomly. Goods or products may experience a change of storage location. Storage can also be done in any place possible.

3. Class-based storage Third, the method of storing goods in the next warehouse is the class based storage method. This method is a combined method of dedicated storage and randomized storage. The class-based storage method is to arrange the location of the storage of goods in the warehouse more flexibly but still neatly.

4. Shared storage Fourth, the use of the shared storage method, which is a method of storing goods in the warehouse which aims to reduce the need for storage space for goods. The shared storage method prioritizes the advantage of differences in the storage time of goods in the warehouse. In this method it is important to determine the items carefully based on the storage space.

5. Cube per order index policy Finally, there is a method of storing goods in the last warehouse, which is the cube per order index policy. The cube per order index policy method is a way of storing goods in the warehouse by paying attention to the level of storage space requirements in the warehouse with the number of product transactions. The cube per order index policy method means setting product locations based on product categories and product movement distances..
